Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  2. 3 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  5. 1 1/4 cups milk
  6. 1 egg

Steps

Steps 1

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in center and pour milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Steps 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. and scoop the batter onto the griddle or frying pan and cook.
